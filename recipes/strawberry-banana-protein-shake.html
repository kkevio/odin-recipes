What is a Strawberry Banana Protein Shake?

This protein shake contains strawberrys, bananas, milk and most importantly your whey protein.

This drink is easily customizable to use any type of fruits and milk and be drank anytime during the day and very easy to make!

Ingredients

(For one person)

  • 1 cup of milk.(any)
  • (can be adjusted to your liking) 1 Scoop of your whey protein powder.
  • 2 cups ice cube
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 large banana
  • (Optional) 1 tablespoon of peanut butter

Steps to make Strawberry Banana Protein Shake

  1. Pour mlik into blender
  2. Pour protein powder into blender (if chunky blend it first)
  3. Drop ice cubes.
  4. Drop strawberries and Bananas.
  5. Add peanut butter. (optional)
  6. Blend until creamy and smooth.
