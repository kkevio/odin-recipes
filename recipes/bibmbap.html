Bibmbap (Korean Rice Bowl)

What is Bibmbap?

Bibmbap (pronounced "bee-BIM-bop") means mixed rice, this dish consist of steamed white rice with marinated vegetables along with raw or seared meat and if preferred with an egg aswell.

This dish is very customizable and can be altered however as you can use any types of meat and will always have a balance between proteins and your greens and will always work to tailor your own taste buds.
